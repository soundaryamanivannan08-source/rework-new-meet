About Stackly

We are building the future of remote team collaboration and high-fidelity video meetings.

Privacy by Default

We don't sell your data, and we don't train external models on your private conversations. Everything you discuss is secured with AES-256 bit end-to-end encryption.

Uncompromising Speed

Latency kills natural conversation. We engineered our proprietary WebRTC routing network to guarantee sub-20ms audio delivery.

Design as a Feature

Enterprise software doesn't have to be ugly. A beautiful intuitive interface reduces cognitive load and makes work enjoyable.

WHO WE ARE

Crafting the Future of Remote Collaboration

We are a fully-distributed team of network engineers, cryptographers, and design purists obsessed with latency, security, and aesthetics.

"We don't build software just to solve a business utility; we build it to restore the joy of human connection across digital spaces."

Stackly started in 2023 with a simple hypothesis: that physical separation shouldn't degrade collaborative flow. We believe that with high-enough sensory fidelity, zero-knowledge privacy, and zero input lag, remote collaboration can feel even more productive than working in the same room.

Asynchronous First

We work in self-directed flow states, respecting deep concentration. We document decisions thoroughly and communicate intentionally.

Extreme Craftsmanship

Every line of code and interface element is designed with intent. We don't take shortcuts when it comes to rendering performance or security.

Human-Centric Focus

We build tools that conform to human psychology, not the other way around. Our products feel light, natural, and low-friction.

GREEN ROUTING

Powering Global Connection Sustainably

We believe bridging physical distances shouldn't cost the Earth. Stackly's server routing networks run on sustainable, high-efficiency architectures.

Traditional video routing systems burn massive energy overhead by keeping regional proxy instances fully spinning even when idle. Stackly’s dynamic resource allocations spin resources down instantly when sessions terminate, minimizing server heat and operational carbon footprints.

100% Renewable Sourcing

Our core edge routing server systems are located in energy-neutral hosting clusters running entirely on hydro, wind, or solar arrays.

Optimized User Rendering

Our custom client WebRTC decoding algorithms decrease device CPU load by 25%, saving battery power and reducing local device carbon generation.

Reforestation Partnerships

We actively offset the transmission carbon overhead of every video conference hosted on Stackly through local global planting campaigns.
